He is a named Co-Investigator on the **DOE Genesis Mission SpectrumFM** proposal (RFA-0003612, Topic 14, Focus Area A), where the project narrative explicitly states: *"Benson (USF; $33,309) **leads agentic-AI tooling**"* (proposal §3, Activity 1).

He is therefore not a junior onboarding student — he is a senior collaborator whose role is to bring agentic-AI architecture into both the existing lens-discovery program and the proposed SpectrumFM work.

When working with him, frame astronomy and physics background tutorially (he is a CS/ML expert without deep astro background), but treat agentic-AI, ML infrastructure, and multi-agent design as his home turf — proposals there should be concrete (architectures, role decompositions, tool surfaces), not introductory.
