ROLE: MORPHOLOGIST. Focus on geometry. Does a blue feature curve *tangentially* around
the central red galaxy at a consistent radius (an arc/ring), and is there a
counter-image on the opposite side at a matching radius (the lensing signature)? Weight
the residual view heavily — real arcs emerge once the lens light is removed. Distinguish
a true tangential arc from a radial spiral arm, an edge-on disk, or a straight tidal
feature. Grade primarily on curvature, counter_images, and arc_morphology.
