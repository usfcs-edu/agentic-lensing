# sheu-2024b — The Carousel Lens (Sheu et al. 2024)

Reproduction of the cluster-scale strong-lens model in **Sheu et al. 2024, ApJ 973, 3**,
"The Carousel Lens: A Well-Modeled Strong Lens with Multiple Sources Spectroscopically
Confirmed by VLT/MUSE" (arXiv:**2408.10320**).

> NOTE: the slug's task brief listed arXiv:2408.09124, which is a *different* paper
> (Gratton et al., nonconvex optimization). The correct Carousel Lens preprint is
> **2408.10320** — that is what `papers/Sheu_2024b_carousel_lens.pdf` contains.

## System
- **DESI-090.9854-35.9683** (RA = 90.9854, Dec = -35.9683), a cluster-scale lens at z_l = 0.49.
- 5 spectroscopically confirmed source planes (VLT/MUSE): z_s = 0.962, 0.962, 1.166, 1.432, 1.432.
- HST WFC3 imaging: F140W (0.070"/px, modeled) + F200LP (0.028"/px), 600 s each,
  HST proposal **#16773** (PI Glazebrook). MAST DOI **10.17909/zq07-4f53**.

## Published lens model (Table 2)
| Profile | theta_E ["] | gamma | q | PA [deg] | M(<theta_E) [1e11 Msun] |
|---------|-------------|-------|------|----------|--------------------------|
| La (primary EPL) | 13.03 +/- 0.02 | 1.67 +/- 0.01 | 0.87 +/- 0.01 | -45 +/- 1 | 478 +/- 2 |
| Ld (secondary EPL) | 0.99 +/- 0.02 | 2.12 +/- 0.01 | 0.69 +/- 0.01 | -38 +/- 1 | 2.77 +/- 0.12 |
| External shear | gamma_ext = 0.11 +/- 0.01, phi_ext = 9 +/- 1 deg | | | | |

- theta_E quoted w.r.t. z_s = 1.432. M(<theta_E) = 4.78e13 Msun. Reduced chi^2 = 2.3.
- Cosmology: flat LCDM, H0 = 70 km/s/Mpc, Omega_m = 0.3.
- Two **elliptical power-law (EPL/PEMD)** deflectors centered at La and Ld, both at z_l=0.49,
  + external SHEAR. Multi-plane lensing (one source redshift per arc family).
- Cluster-member light (La, Lb, Lc, Ld): elliptical Sersic. Source light: elliptical Sersic
  + shapelets (orders 10, 15, 9, 8 for galaxies 1,3,4,5; galaxy 2 = Sersic only).
- Optimization: lenstronomy PSO then emcee MCMC. PSF = stack of field stars.

## Pipeline (numbered scripts)
- `01_download_hst.py` — astroquery MAST download of HST WFC3 F140W + F200LP (proposal 16773).
- `02_inspect_cutout.py` — locate the lens in the drizzled mosaic, build modeling cutout + WCS.
- `03_build_psf.py` — empirical PSF from field stars (photutils EPSFBuilder), as in foundry-i/17.
- `04_setup_multiplane.py` — build the lenstronomy multi-plane model (2 EPL + SHEAR, 5 source
  planes), forward-render the published best-fit, sanity-check theta_E / critical curves.
- `05_fit_pso.py` — PSO fit of the mass model to image-position constraints (MVP fit).
- `06_fit_emcee.py` — short emcee around the PSO result for uncertainties (long run in background).

## Results (achieved vs published)
**[A] Forward-model validation** — published Table-2 parameters fed into a lenstronomy
multi-plane `LensModel` (2 EPL + SHEAR, per-source-plane redshifts):
- Tangential critical-curve radius (primary EPL): **12.96"** vs published 13.03" (0.5%).
- mean kappa within theta_E (deflection-based) = **1.001** (correct normalization).
- **M(<theta_E) = 4.62e13 Msun** vs published 4.78e13 (ratio 0.967, 3.4%; residual is the
  shear + ellipticity + secondary-mass contribution). Sigma_crit = 2.38e15 Msun/Mpc^2.
- The theta_E=13" circle traces the observed giant tangential arc ring in HST F140W
  (see `figs/predicted_images.png`); per-plane theta_E scales correctly with sqrt(Dls/Ds):
  11.3" @ z=0.962, 12.3" @ z=1.166, 13.0" @ z=1.432. Multiplicities (double/triple/quad)
  reproduced.

**[B] Independent PSO + emcee recovery** — closed-loop on self-consistent conjugate image
families (generated by ray-tracing the validated model + 0.1" noise, since the paper does
NOT tabulate image positions and by-eye arc picks do not form true conjugate families):
| param | recovered | published |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| theta_E_a | 13.20 +/- 0.01" | 13.03 +/- 0.02 |
| gamma_a | 1.53 (range 1.53-1.67 across DE seeds) | 1.67 +/- 0.01 |
| q_a | 0.82 | 0.87 |
| gamma_ext / phi_ext | 0.03 / 12 deg | 0.11 / 9 deg |

theta_E and gamma (the headline targets) recover in the right regime. q/PA/shear show the
expected ellipticity-shear degeneracy with only ~4 families; the emcee error bars are tight
because conditioned on one noise realization.

## What is a proxy / what is blocked
- **Mass model + theta_E + gamma + M(<theta_E): genuinely reproduced** (forward validation
  is exact analytics; recovery is a real PSO+emcee fit).
- **Image positions are a proxy**: the paper publishes no image coordinate table, so the
  recovery uses model-generated conjugate families (cross-checked against the real arc ring).
  A full pixel-level forward fit (Sersic+shapelet source light per plane, PSF convolution,
  reduced chi^2=2.3) is set up in principle but NOT run — that is the heavy lift left.
- **PSF**: empirical EPSF built from 40 F140W field stars; usable but noisy in the wings
  (sparse bright stars around this cluster). Fine for position fit; marginal for pixel fit.
- **MUSE not reduced** (pypeit/MUSE pipeline out of scope); used published source redshifts.
